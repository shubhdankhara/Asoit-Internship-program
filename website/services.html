What We Offer

Exceptional experiences crafted around the world's finest coffee.

01

Artisan Roastery

Our in-house roastery is the heart of Caffe. Using a vintage Probat drum roaster, our Master Roaster Arjun Mehta works in small batches — typically 10–15 kg — to ensure every roast reaches its full potential.

We roast every morning before the café opens. This means the coffee in your cup was roasted within the last 48 hours, not weeks ago like supermarket brands. You can taste the difference immediately.

We offer four distinct roast profiles: Light (bright & floral), Medium (balanced), Medium-Dark (caramel & nutty), and Dark (bold & smoky). All available for purchase in-store and online.

Daily Fresh Roast 4 Roast Profiles Small Batch Single Origin
02

Private Events

Our private lounge on the upper floor can be exclusively reserved for corporate gatherings, product launches, intimate celebrations, or creative workshops. The space seats up to 40 guests and features its own dedicated bar.

Every private event comes with a personalised coffee menu, a dedicated host barista, and optional catering from our kitchen. We've hosted everything from startup pitch nights to anniversary dinners.

We also offer bespoke coffee experiences for events — live brewing stations, cupping ceremonies, and cocktail pairings that make your gathering truly unforgettable.

Up to 40 Guests Dedicated Barista Custom Menu AV Setup Included
03

Barista Academy

Learn from India's finest baristas. Our structured training programmes range from beginner workshops — perfect for coffee lovers who want to brew better at home — to SCA-certified professional courses.

Classes are intentionally small (max 6 students) so every participant gets hands-on time on our commercial equipment. You'll learn espresso theory, milk texturing, grind calibration, and sensory evaluation.

Our Saturday Morning Cupping is a community favourite — a free weekly session where we taste and compare 4 different coffees, hosted by Arjun himself. Open to all, no booking required.

SCA Certified Courses Max 6 Per Class Beginner to Pro Free Saturday Cupping
04

Coffee Subscription

Never run out of exceptional coffee. Our subscription service delivers freshly roasted beans to your door within 24 hours of roasting — across Ahmedabad, Surat, Mumbai, and Bangalore.

Every month, you can choose your beans, roast level, grind size, and quantity. Subscribers also get early access to seasonal micro-lots and limited single origins that sell out quickly in-store.

Pause, skip, or cancel anytime with zero friction. Our subscribers consistently tell us it's the best coffee decision they've ever made.

24h After Roasting Fully Customisable Pan-India Delivery Easy Pause / Cancel

Common Questions

Do I need any prior experience for the Foundation class? +
Absolutely none. The Foundation session is designed for complete beginners. We cover everything from scratch — the only requirement is a love of great coffee and curiosity to learn.
Can I visit the roastery during my café visit? +
Yes! Our roastery is open to guests Tuesday through Sunday between 9am–11am when roasting is underway. Just ask any of our team and they'll take you through. No booking needed.
How do I book the private lounge for an event? +
Use our contact form or call us directly. We'll send you a detailed proposal within 24 hours. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for weekday events, and 4 weeks for weekends.
What's included in the coffee subscription? +
Subscribers receive 250g or 500g bags of freshly roasted whole beans or ground coffee, delivered on a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly cadence. Each delivery includes a tasting note card describing the origin and flavour profile.
Is the Saturday Morning Cupping really free? +
Completely free. It's our way of giving back to the community that has supported us for 27 years. It runs every Saturday at 9:30am and lasts about 90 minutes. You're welcome to buy coffee afterwards, but there's no obligation whatsoever.
