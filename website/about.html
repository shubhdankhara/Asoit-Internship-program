Our Story

From a humble passion to a city landmark — 27 years of craft.

Where Coffee Becomes Art

Founded in 1998 by Master Roaster Arjun Mehta, Caffe began as a small 12-seat espresso bar in Ahmedabad's Artisanal District. The vision was radical for its time: treat every cup as a culinary creation deserving the same reverence as fine cuisine.

Today, we occupy a restored 1930s colonial building, serve over 400 guests daily, and maintain direct relationships with 11 farms across three continents. Yet the spirit remains unchanged — every single cup still gets our full attention.

We were Ahmedabad's first specialty coffee bar, first to introduce single-origin pour-overs, and first to host public cupping sessions — a tradition we continue every Saturday morning.

Our Journey

1998

The First Cup

Arjun Mehta opens a 12-seat espresso bar with a single La Marzocco machine and beans imported directly from Yirgacheffe, Ethiopia.

2003

The Roastery Opens

We install our own Probat drum roaster, becoming one of India's first micro-roasteries to roast fresh daily on-site.

2009

Farm Direct Program

Launch of our direct trade model, forging lasting relationships with farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Brazil — cutting out middlemen entirely.

2014

National Recognition

Named "Best Specialty Café in Western India" by the India Coffee Guide. Barista Priya Sharma wins the National Latte Art Championship.

2019

The Grand Expansion

Renovation of the 1930s colonial building to create our current flagship — tripling our capacity while restoring every original arch and terrazzo floor.

2026

Today & Beyond

Launching our Barista Academy and a new subscription coffee program, delivering our roasts to homes across India within 24 hours of roasting.

What We Believe

We approach every brew method as a discipline. Our baristas train for months before serving their first cup. Precision, patience, and passion are non-negotiable.

From hosting local artists to partnering with neighbourhood businesses, Caffe is deeply woven into Ahmedabad's cultural fabric. We're not just a café — we're a gathering place.

Where Our Beans Come From

Yirgacheffe, Ethiopia

Our longest partnership — 18 years with the Washed Heirloom Cooperative. These beans carry bright jasmine and blueberry notes, characteristic of the region's ancient Arabica varietals grown above 2,000 metres.

Floral · Citrus · Bright

Huila, Colombia

From the steep volcanic slopes of the Andes, our Colombian beans offer a rich caramel sweetness with hints of red apple and a silky, balanced body — perfect as the backbone of our house espresso blend.

Caramel · Apple · Balanced

Cerrado, Brazil

Our Brazilian natural-processed beans are sun-dried on raised beds for 30 days, developing an intensely chocolatey, nutty profile with low acidity — beloved by guests who prefer a smooth, classic cup.

Chocolate · Hazelnut · Smooth
