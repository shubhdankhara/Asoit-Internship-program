The Classic Cappuccino

A masterpiece of balance. Our cappuccino is prepared with a rich double espresso base, beautifully combined with silky steamed milk and topped with a thick, velvety layer of foam.

Master Recipe: 1 part Espresso, 1 part Steamed Milk, 1 part Milk Foam. Served in a 6oz ceramic cup.

From Farm to Cup

01

Cultivation

Young coffee trees are planted in shaded nurseries before moving to the farm for 3-4 years.

02

Harvesting

Only the ripest red cherries are hand-picked at the peak of their sugar content.

03

Processing

The pulp is removed and beans are dried naturally in the sun or through washing.

04

Roasting

Green beans are heated at 400°F to unlock their distinctive aroma and flavor profile.

The Master Collection

Espresso

The foundation. A concentrated shot of coffee with a rich crema.

Recipe: 18g coffee, 36g output, 28-32 seconds.

Latte

Smooth and creamy. Espresso mixed with plenty of steamed milk.

Recipe: 1 shot espresso, 8-10oz steamed milk, light foam.

Flat White

The micro-foam specialist. Rich espresso with velvety thin milk.

Recipe: Double ristretto, 5-6oz micro-foam milk.

Cortado

Perfectly balanced. Equal parts espresso and warm milk.

Recipe: 2oz espresso, 2oz steamed milk. No foam.

Americano

Classic strength. Espresso shots topped with hot water.

Recipe: 2 shots espresso, 6oz hot water.

Cafe Au Lait

French tradition. Strong brewed coffee with warm milk.

Recipe: 50% Brewed Coffee, 50% Steamed Milk.
